FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

Q: What services does JC Photography Studio offer?

JC Photography Studio offers professional photography, videography, and editing services for a wide range of events, including weddings, debut, birthdays, and other events.

Q: How can I contact JC Photography Studio?

You can contact us via phone at 09777375223 or by email at jcphotographystudio11@gmail.com. Our office is also open for in-person consultations by appointment.

Q: What makes JC Photography Studio unique?

What sets us apart is our commitment to capturing unique and authentic moments, utilizing state-of-the-art equipment, and providing personalized services tailored to each client's needs.

Q: Do you offer a consultation before booking?

Yes, we offer complimentary consultations to discuss your specific requirements, answer any questions, and ensure we understand your vision for the event.

Q: How do I book a photography session with JC Photography Studio?

To book a session, simply visit our facebook page at https://www.facebook.com/jcphotography11 and message us the details. We'll get in touch with you to discuss details and confirm availability.

Q: Do you provide videography services?

Yes, we offer professional videography services to capture the essence of your event. Our team uses the latest equipment to produce cinematic and memorable videos.

Q: Can I customize my videography package?

Absolutely! We understand that each event is unique. We offer customizable videography packages to suit your specific needs and preferences.

Q: Do you offer editing services for photos and videos?

Yes, we provide professional editing services to enhance the quality of both photos and videos. Our team ensures a polished and cohesive final product.

Q: Can I request specific edits or enhancements?

Certainly! We welcome your input and specific requests for edits. Our goal is to deliver a product that aligns with your vision and expectations.

Q: How long does the editing process take?

The editing timeline depends on the scope of the project. We aim to deliver a preview of edited images within 2 - 3 days and the final edited collection within a week.

Q: What software do you use for editing?

We use industry-standard editing software to ensure the highest quality output. Our team is proficient in Preimere Pro for both photo and video editing.
